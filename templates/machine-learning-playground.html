Summary

The MMR presented in the graph above is based on averages of public health measures from the years 2009-2019. The interactive form allows users to input their own values for public health measures to explore a measure's effect on MMR. The table below shows possible values for health measures that users might consider entering.

Health Measure Description Minimum Average Maximum
Diabetes Percentage of adults who reported being told they have diabetes. 5.3 9.7 16.2
Premature Death Number of years of potential life lost before age 75 per 100,000 population. 5,345 7,546 11,485
Physical Inactivity Percentage of adults who reported no physical activity or exercise other than their job in the past 30 days. 15.7 24.6 38.1
Low Birthweight Percentage of infants weighing less than 2,500 grams. 5.6 8.1 12.3
High Health Status - Female Percentage of females who reported their health as very good or excellent. 39.3 52.1 64.1
Note: All terms and description bases on 2019 HWC Report (unless otherwise stated in description). The most recent data available are used. For some measures, multiple years of data are combined to have sufficient sample size (America's Health Rankings, 2021).
